## Battle Master Maneuvers
**Superiority Die:** 1d8

### Riposte
*Reaction · Cost: 1 Superiority Die*

When an enemy misses you with a melee attack, use your reaction to make one melee weapon attack against them. Add the superiority die to the damage roll.

↳ **Best for:** Counterattacking after a dodge.

---

### Commander's Strike
*Bonus Action · Cost: 1 Superiority Die*

Forgo one of your attacks. Use your bonus action to direct an ally — they immediately make one attack as a reaction and add the superiority die to the damage roll.

↳ **Best for:** Letting the rogue fish for a free crit on your turn.

## Backstory

Once a sergeant in the **Ironclad Legion** — a mercenary company known for brutal efficiency. His squad was his family until a botched ambush left him the only survivor.

He wanders now. A washed-up veteran drowning guilt in cheap ale and half-hearted attempts to keep new "friends" alive. He joined this party on a whim — maybe a drunken dare — but he's *determined* to do better this time.

He'll grumble. He'll insult. He'll act like an asshole. But if push comes to shove? He takes the hit. He pulls the watch. He *dies* before he lets the party fall apart again.
